FLOWER
Nature refers to the general realm of living plants and animals, and in some cases to the processes associated with inanimate objects. It encompasses all the animals, plants, rocks, etc. in the world and all the features, forces, and processes that happen or exist independently of people. This includes the physical world and everything in it, such as plants, animals, mountains, oceans, stars, etc. that is not made by people.
